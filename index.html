About Us

Van life is very common in western countries. So in this channel at E Bull Jet, we are featuring van life in India. This channel will showcase the real-life in India and nearby countries. We are ebin and Libin, siblings belonging to Iritty, a locality in Kannur District of Kerala, India. We aim to explore India and nearby countries in a van that's converted into a home. In this journey, we will sleep, cook, and travel in our van and will ex cover places at minimal expenses.

We would like to bring our Van life though this Website - E BULL JET

Mission

Our goal has always been to make the outdoors more accessible to everyone, but it is clear that this community needs more than that.
